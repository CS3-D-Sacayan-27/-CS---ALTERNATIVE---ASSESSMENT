UGNAYAN 2022

COMPETITIONS


Congratulations to CLUSTER 2: RED ABUYO on achieving impressive results during the PSHS System UGNAYAN 2022! RED ABUYO is a cluster of campuses from Caraga, Cagayan Valley, CALABARZON, Western Visayas. A big shout-out to all our athletes and staff who have performed exceptionally, securing us the Overall Champion title. Their proud achievements include scooping seven (7) gold medals, six (6) silver medals, and seven (7) bronze medals out of twenty (20) events held in Baguio City from November 7 to 11, 2022.
