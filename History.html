HISTORY

History & Beginnings

The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus (PSHS-CRC) is the 13th campus of the Philippine Science High School System, situated in Brgy. Ampayon, Butuan. The school admits and awards scholarships to students who excel in science and math. It serves scholars from Caraga provinces, which include Surigao del Norte, Surigao del Sur, Dinagat Islands, Agusan del Norte, and Agusan del Sur. Some scholars are from Region X's neighboring provinces, while others come from other parts of the country. The PSHS-CRC officially launched in June 2014.
The family of former Philippine Vice President Teofisto T. Guingona Sr. gave a five-hectare plot of land to the Philippine Science High School on April 1, 2013. Due to a detected geohazard at the proposed location, the City Government of Butuan, led by former Mayor Dr. Ferdinand Amante, offered a new 5-hectare plot of land in the DO Plaza Government Center, Barangay Ampayon, Butuan City. Dr. Ruwina S. Gonzaga, the Campus's first Campus Director, oversaw the operation, which began at Caraga State University's Main Campus in the same barangay.
