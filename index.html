Philippine Science High School Caraga Region Campus


The Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus (PSHS - CRC) is the 13th Campus of the PSHS System managed by the Department of Science and Technology. Regarded as one of the best and most prominent schools in the country, PSHS-CRC provides high-quality education and a wide range of options that allows motivated students to pursue their desired careers, with an evident emphasis of Science, Technology, Engineering, & Mathematics.
